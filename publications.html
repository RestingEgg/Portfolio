Publications and Presentations



    1. "Total Quality Management" - ICTEST 2020

      This paper provides a detailed analysis on the nuances of Total Quality Management, which is a renowned practice of managing and grooming employees, who may be working for a public or private organisation. This practice ensures that the employees regularly update themselves, work on developing their skills and knowledge, and the quality of yield, to constantly provide customer satisfacetion and produce goods and services which are actually very valuable in the market.

      I wrote this paper under the guidance of Dr. Remya Lathabhava (SMEC) Sr. Professor - VIT Vellore

    2. "Study of Growing Significance of Education in India" - RER2021

      This paper is written, keeping in mind the importance of education for the inhabitants aka. contributors of our country, and how this factor can help in the rise of our nation. After due consideration to the factors affecting education in India, and curating the data on literacy rate and ration in our country, an analysis was made on how the education in our country was established and how it got affected. Importance of education was also highlighted, and how quality education and nurturing, when provided to all, will work wonders on our country.

      I wrote this paper under the guidance of Dr.Bangalore Morarji (Dept. Social Sci.) Sr.Professor - VIT Vellore
